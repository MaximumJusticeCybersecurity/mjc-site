# Campaign Document Control

## Purpose

This file defines the document system for any future campaign for municipal, county, state, or federal office.

## Core Rule

All verified election research, filing requirements, official forms, deadlines, candidate records, opponent research, decisions, and campaign artifacts must be stored in this repository with source attribution and a last-verified date.

## Required Folders

- `research/offices/` — office-specific eligibility, powers, incumbents, election history, and P-win analysis
- `research/sources/` — source register and official links
- `legal/eligibility/` — residency, age, citizenship, and office-specific qualification records
- `legal/ballot-access/` — nomination petitions, nomination papers, signature rules, filing windows, objections, and withdrawal rules
- `legal/compliance/` — campaign finance, ethics, conflicts, reporting, and disclaimers
- `operations/calendar/` — election calendar, filing deadlines, events, and internal milestones
- `operations/forms/` — required forms, checklists, and submission packages
- `operations/petitions/` — petition strategy, circulator instructions, signature tracking, and quality control
- `operations/volunteers/` — volunteer roles, training, and contact-management rules
- `strategy/` — race selection, pathway, P-win, message, field, finance, and coalition strategy
- `communications/` — approved public language, speeches, press, digital, and issue messaging
- `decisions/` — dated decision records and rationale
- `agent-handoffs/` — clear assignments, status, blockers, and next actions

## Document Standards

Every research document must include:

1. Office or topic
2. Jurisdiction
3. Election year
4. Verified facts
5. Assumptions or unknowns
6. Official sources
7. Last verified date
8. Owner or agent responsible
9. Next action

## Privacy Rule

Do not store private family information, medical information, account credentials, unredacted identity documents, or campaign-sensitive donor data in the public repository.

## Change Control

- No unsupported claims.
- Corrections must replace inaccurate statements and document the correction.
- Election-law information must be reverified against official sources before action.
- Draft nomination petitions must never be represented as official forms.
