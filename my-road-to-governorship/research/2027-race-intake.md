## Ground Rules

- Use official election records as the primary source.
- Distinguish verified facts, inference, and unknowns.
- Do not publish private family or personal information.
- Do not collect nomination signatures before the lawful circulation period or on unofficial forms.
- A public expression-of-interest list is not a nomination petition and must never be represented as one.
