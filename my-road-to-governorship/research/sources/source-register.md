## Source Rules

1. Prefer official election records, statutes, meeting minutes, campaign-finance filings, and government records.
2. Use news reporting for context, not as the sole source for legal or electoral facts.
3. Record exact vote totals from certified results whenever available.
4. Distinguish a declared candidate from a rumored or possible candidate.
5. Reverify all deadlines and forms in the election year.
